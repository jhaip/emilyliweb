Emily Li, 16, started to study vocal from Sally Gu since she was 9 year old. She started to sing at various stages and venues in east coast of the United States since 2010. She won the Silver Medal at the 2013 “Beijing Water Cube” Overseas Chinese Youth Singing Competition held on August 8, 2013 in Beijing, China; won the First Place at the 2013 “Beijing Water Cube” Overseas Chinese Youth Singing Competition New York Division held on June 9, 2013 in Flushing, New York;
